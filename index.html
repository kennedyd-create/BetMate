How BetMate Works

1. The Bankroll

Everyone starts the month with exactly BM$ 100. You cannot top up or add more money during the month. Manage your bankroll wisely!

2. Blind Pools

Create a challenge on an upcoming match and set a wager. Your pick is hidden from the lobby. Other players can see the pool size and join in blindly. Picks are only revealed to you once you lock in your own wager.

3. Winner-Takes-All

  • The player(s) with the correct pick take the entire pool.
  • If multiple players win, the pool is split proportionally based on their wagers.
  • If everyone is wrong, or everyone picks the exact same outcome, all wagers are safely refunded.

4. End of Month Settlement

At the end of the month, the Commissioner runs the settlement. BetMate calculates exactly who is in profit and who is in the red compared to the starting BM$ 100. Losers pay out to winners in real life, and the app resets everyone to BM$ 100 for the new month.
